Family Hub

Created to serve the English speaking international community of families living in the Haarlemmermeer area of The Netherlands.

Family Hub was designed to make a difference it to newcomers to a country to be able to get out and connected to the community around. The Family Hub goal is to inform and connect English speaking families to the events and activities available in their local area.
